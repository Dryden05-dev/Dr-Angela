Dr.Angela Colquhoun

MBChB (Pret) FCPaed (SA)

Dr. Angela Colquhoun is a specialist paediatrician at Mediclinic Kloof in Pretoria who combines clinical expertise with gentle, family centred care. She provides a full spectrum of paediatric services from newborn and infant check ups, immunisations, routine child health visits to neonatal and intensive care support when needed. She is committed to staying current with the latest developments in paediatric and neonatal medicine so she can offer children and their families modern, evidence based care with empathy and respect.
